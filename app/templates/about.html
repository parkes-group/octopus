How It Works

Learn how this tool helps you find the cheapest times to charge your battery on Agile Octopus.

What is Agile Octopus?

Agile Octopus is a time-of-use electricity tariff offered by Octopus Energy in the UK. Unlike traditional fixed-rate tariffs, Agile Octopus prices change every 30 minutes based on wholesale electricity market rates.

This means electricity can be:

  • Cheaper during periods of low demand or high renewable generation
  • More expensive during peak demand times
  • Negative on rare occasions when you're paid to consume electricity

Where Does the Data Come From?

All pricing data is fetched directly from Octopus Energy's public API:

  • Data source: https://api.octopus.energy/v1/
  • Update frequency: Every 30 minutes (half-hourly pricing)
  • Coverage: All 14 UK electricity regions
  • Pricing: Includes VAT, displayed in pence per kWh

We cache API responses for 5 minutes to reduce load on Octopus Energy's servers while ensuring data freshness.

Who Is This Tool For?

This tool is designed for UK households who:

  • Are on or considering the Agile Octopus tariff
  • Have home battery storage (e.g., Tesla Powerwall, GivEnergy, Solax)
  • Have solar panels (optional, but common)
  • Want to optimise charging times to minimise electricity costs

The tool helps you identify the cheapest times to charge your battery, potentially saving money on your electricity bills.

What Can You Do?

  • Find the lowest 30-minute price: Identify the single cheapest half-hour slot
  • Calculate cheapest charging blocks: Find the best continuous period for charging (0.5 to 6 hours)
  • Estimate costs: Calculate how much it will cost to charge your battery
  • View price charts: See price trends throughout the day

  • Always verify current prices with Octopus Energy before making decisions
  • This tool is not affiliated with Octopus Energy
  • Actual savings depend on many factors including your usage patterns, battery capacity, and tariff terms
