How Agile Pricing Works

Agile Pricing helps UK households understand Agile Octopus electricity prices and identify the cheapest times to use electricity, particularly for charging home batteries, electric vehicles, and other flexible appliances.

By analysing half-hourly pricing data from Octopus Energy, the tool highlights low-cost and negative-price periods so you can make more informed decisions about when to use energy.

Who Is This Tool For?

Agile Pricing is designed for UK households who:

  • Are on, or considering, the Agile Octopus tariff
  • Have home battery storage (such as Tesla Powerwall, GivEnergy, Solax, etc.)
  • May also have solar panels (optional but common)
  • Want to reduce electricity costs by using power at cheaper times

The tool helps you quickly identify the best times to charge batteries or run energy-intensive appliances.

What Can You Do With Agile Pricing?

Using this tool, you can:

  • Find the lowest 30-minute price of the day
  • Calculate the cheapest charging blocks, from 30 minutes up to 6 hours
  • Estimate charging costs based on selected time windows
  • View interactive price charts showing half-hourly trends throughout the day
  • Compare regional prices to understand how costs vary across the UK

What Is Agile Octopus?

Agile Octopus is a time-of-use electricity tariff offered by Octopus Energy in the UK. Unlike traditional fixed or standard variable tariffs, Agile Octopus prices change every 30 minutes based on wholesale electricity market conditions.

This means electricity prices can be:

  • Cheaper during periods of low demand or high renewable generation
  • More expensive during peak demand times
  • Negative on rare occasions, meaning you may be paid to use electricity

Agile Octopus rewards households that can shift electricity usage to cheaper periods.

Where Does the Data Come From?

All pricing data shown on this site comes directly from Octopus Energy's official public API.

  • Data source: Octopus Energy public API
  • Update frequency: Every 30 minutes (half-hourly pricing)
  • Coverage: All 14 UK electricity regions
  • Pricing: Unit rates include VAT and are shown in pence per kWh

To balance accuracy and performance, API responses are cached for 15 minutes, ensuring data remains fresh while minimising unnecessary requests to Octopus Energy's servers.
