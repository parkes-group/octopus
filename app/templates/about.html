How Agile Pricing Helps You Find the Cheapest Octopus Energy Prices

Agile Pricing helps UK households on the Agile Octopus tariff find the cheapest half-hourly electricity prices each day, making it easier to reduce energy bills by shifting usage to lower-cost periods.

By analysing official Octopus Energy pricing data in near real time, the tool highlights the cheapest blocks of time to use electricity — ideal for charging home batteries, electric vehicles, and running flexible or energy-intensive appliances when prices are lowest.

Who Is Agile Pricing For?

Agile Pricing is designed for UK households who:

  • Are on, or considering, the Agile Octopus electricity tariff
  • Have home battery storage (such as Tesla Powerwall, GivEnergy, Solax, etc.)
  • May also have solar panels (optional but common)
  • Want to reduce electricity costs by using power at cheaper times

The tool helps you quickly identify the best times of day to charge batteries, charge EVs, or run appliances while avoiding expensive peak periods.

What Can You Do With Agile Pricing Tools?

Using Agile Pricing, you can:

  • Find the cheapest 30-minute electricity price of the day
  • Identify the cheapest continuous charging blocks, from 30 minutes up to 6 hours
  • See the cheapest remaining time windows later in the day
  • Estimate electricity costs based on selected time periods
  • View interactive charts showing half-hourly price changes
  • Compare prices across different UK electricity regions

How Much Can You Save With Agile Pricing?

Electricity prices on the Agile Octopus tariff can vary significantly throughout the day. By shifting usage to the cheapest half-hourly periods, many households are able to reduce electricity costs compared to flat or standard variable tariffs.

Agile Pricing makes these low-cost windows easy to spot, helping you plan battery charging, EV charging, and flexible appliance use during the cheapest blocks of time — and avoid the most expensive peak hours.

What Is the Agile Octopus Electricity Tariff?

Agile Octopus is a time-of-use electricity tariff offered by Octopus Energy in the UK. Unlike traditional fixed or standard variable tariffs, Agile Octopus prices change every 30 minutes based on wholesale electricity market conditions.

This means electricity prices can be:

  • Cheaper during periods of low demand or high renewable generation
  • More expensive during peak demand times
  • Negative on rare occasions, meaning you may be paid to use electricity

Agile Octopus rewards households that can shift electricity usage to cheaper periods.

Where Does Agile Pricing Data Come From?

All electricity pricing data shown on Agile Pricing comes directly from Octopus Energy’s official public API.

  • Data source: Octopus Energy public API
  • Update frequency: Every 30 minutes (half-hourly pricing)
  • Coverage: All 14 UK electricity regions
  • Pricing: Unit rates include VAT and are shown in pence per kWh

To balance accuracy and performance, API responses are cached briefly, ensuring prices stay up to date while minimising unnecessary requests to Octopus Energy’s servers.
