Export Tariffs

If you generate electricity (e.g. from solar panels) and export it to the grid, Octopus offers tariffs that pay you for the energy you export.

Export tariffs pay you for surplus electricity sent to the grid. The tools on this site help you compare rates for both import (buying) and export (selling).

Choose a Tariff Type

Fixed Export

A flat rate for every unit you export. Predictable — same p/kWh at any time of day.

View Fixed Rates
Agile Outgoing

Half-hourly rates that follow the wholesale market. Can be higher than fixed during peak sun — or negative.

View Agile Rates
🔖 Tip: Select your region on each tariff page to see rates for your area.

Understanding Export Prices

  • VAT: Export rates can be shown including or excluding VAT (5%). Toggle on the tariff pages.
  • Negative prices: Agile Outgoing can go negative when supply exceeds demand — you may be charged to export in those half-hours.
  • Region: Rates vary by UK region. Select your region on each tariff page.
